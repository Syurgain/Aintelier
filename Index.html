About

I draw the moments that don't last long enough.

I'm a hobbyist illustrator from Malaysia. I mostly draw people — a face caught mid-laugh, a summer that's already half-melted, the ordinary warmth that's easy to miss while it's happening.

I post my work as syurgain and keep the rest of me offline. The name is the whole introduction — if a piece here makes you feel the heat of an afternoon you'd half-forgotten, that's the entire point.
